# Élow Estúdio Design — Business Context

## 1. Business Definition

Élow Estúdio Design is a branding studio based in Recife, Pernambuco, Brazil, operating remotely across the country and internationally.

They build visual identity systems for service businesses — not just logos, but complete strategic brand systems. Their central belief: every serious business deserves a brand equal to the quality of what it does. A well-built visual identity is not a luxury. It is the foundation of everything that comes after.

Élow operates as a strategic partner, not a design vendor. They begin with deep discovery and understanding of the business, its audience, and its positioning — then design the visual system from that foundation.

## 3. Core Beliefs

- "Você não precisa de um logo bonito para o Instagram. Você precisa de uma marca estratégica que construa um negócio que banque a vida que você quer."
- A logo alone is not a brand. A brand is a complete system: logo, palette, typography, patterns, and the coherence between them.
- Visual identity is the first communication a business has with its audience. It must be planned to connect correctly.
- 80% of businesses fail before 5 years (Sebrae). Investment in design is a decisive factor for survival and differentiation.
- Strategy before aesthetics. Every visual decision comes from understanding the business — who they are, who they speak to, where they want to go.
- Artisanal care. Every project is treated individually, with attention to detail and genuine commitment to results.

## 6. Methodology — E.L.O.W.

Élow follows a four-phase proprietary methodology:

**E — Empathize (Empatizar)**
Deep listening and understanding of the business, its challenges, and ambitions. Genuine understanding of the client and where they are going.

**L — Lead (Condução do projeto)**
Strategic direction and positioning. Every visual decision is anchored in strategy, not aesthetics alone.

**O — Originate (Criação)**
Intentional creation of the complete visual system. Every element designed to work together and serve the business.

**W — Walk (Caminhar)**
Delivery, implementation, and strategic support. The client receives all files plus a strategic support session to define an action plan.
