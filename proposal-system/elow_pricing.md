## NAMING

### Combo Básico — Naming
3 name options with full research and INPI viability.

**Deliverables:**
- Naming strategy consultation
- 3 final name options with rationale
- INPI trademark availability (up to 2 classes, verified by specialist lawyer)
- Social media username availability
- Internal research/survey with feedback
- Strategic support to choose the ideal name

**Prazo:** 30 dias úteis (~1.5 months)
**Investimento:** R$3.000 à vista | 12x de R$300

> OBS: Projects with more than 2 INPI classes require a custom quote.

### Combo Ideal — Naming

**Deliverables:**
- Everything in Combo Básico
- 5 final name options (instead of 3)

**Prazo:** 30 dias úteis (~1.5 months)

## IDENTIDADE VISUAL

All IV packages include the same core deliverables. The difference is in the applications bundled in.

### Core Deliverables (all IV packages)
- Logotipo Principal
- Logotipo Alternativo (other logo signatures)
- Selo (reduced logo for alternative uses)
- Marca D'água (transparent background for photos and posts)
- Pattern (estampas)
- Elementos visuais
- Paleta de Cores (colour codes)
- Tipografias (main + auxiliary fonts)
- Manual da Marca Completo (brand usage guide)
- Capa para Facebook ou LinkedIn
- Sugestão de foto de perfil para rede social
- Reunião estratégica de fechamento

**Prazo:** minimum 30 dias úteis (~1.5 months)
