## 5. Ideal Client

**Who they serve:**
- Service business where credibility and visual perception are decisive factors
- Has a quality product or service — brand does not yet match that quality
- Open to a proper discovery process before design begins
- Sees brand investment as a business decision, not a design expense

**Who is not a fit:**
- Businesses that want a logo only, no strategy
- Those unwilling to participate in briefing and discovery
- Businesses that see design as a commodity to be priced against competitors

## 6. Voice & Tone

**Tone:**
- Warm and confident, without being salesy
- Strategic and direct
- Artisanal — conveys genuine care and intentionality
- Brazilian warmth combined with professional clarity

**Principles:**
- Be specific to this client — their industry, their clients, their moment
- Frame the situation as an opportunity, not a critique of what they lack
- Be honest about what each package does and does not deliver
- Short sentences. Clean language. No corporate filler.
- The client should feel like a thoughtful expert is speaking to them plainly

**Examples:**

Off: "Oferecemos soluções inovadoras de branding para elevar sua empresa ao próximo nível."
On: "Um negócio sério merece uma marca à altura do que faz."

Off: "Temos uma equipe apaixonada que vai transformar seu negócio."
On: "Cada projeto é tratado de forma artesanal — com atenção ao detalhe e comprometimento genuíno com o resultado."

Off: "Aproveite esta oportunidade incrível!"
On: "Esta proposta é válida por 30 dias."

## 7. Proposal Structure

Every Élow proposal follows this order:

1. **Cover** — Client name, project type, "Proposta válida por 30 dias"
2. **Sumário Executivo** — 3–5 sentences. Acknowledge what the business has built; name the specific gap the brand is not yet filling; state what this proposal delivers.
3. **Contexto do Negócio** — Why visual identity matters specifically for this client's category, clients, and competitive context. Use their specific situation, not generic brand theory.
4. **Nossa Abordagem** — The E.L.O.W. process applied to this project scope. Make it specific to what the project involves.
5. **Investimento** — Present the recommended package clearly. If relevant, show 2–3 options (e.g. Combo Básico vs. Combo Ideal vs. Combo Completo). Always flag the recommended one. Always be honest about what a lower tier does and does not include.
6. **Próximos Passos** — One clear action. Default: a short call to confirm scope and align a start date.
