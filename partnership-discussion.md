# Vyager × Élow — Partnership Review
**May 2026**

---

What started as a marketing engagement has become something more involved — and more valuable — for both businesses. Before we move into the next phase, we want to make sure the terms we're working under actually reflect that.

This document is not a formal agreement. It is our way of putting our thinking on the table so we can align and move forward with clarity.

---

## Where we are

This collaboration started with a clear scope: Vyager manages marketing and paid traffic for Élow. Over time, and because the opportunity was there, it grew into something more hands-on. We have been involved in sales calls, proposal structure, scripts, lead follow-up, and commercial strategy.

The original terms no longer describe what is actually happening — and that is worth sitting down and correcting together.

---

## What Vyager brings to this collaboration

**Paid traffic and campaign management**
- Google Ads setup, management, and ongoing optimisation
- Budget allocation and performance monitoring
- Campaign adjustments based on lead quality, not just volume

**Commercial and sales support**
- WhatsApp lead follow-up and initial contact
- Meeting scheduling and pipeline management
- Participation in sales meetings alongside the Élow team
- Proposal structure and copy
- Sales scripts tailored to Élow's services and client profile
- Objection handling and closing support

**Strategy and direction**
- Monthly performance reviews
- Commercial strategy input — what to focus on, what to pause, why
- Guidance on pricing, positioning, and deal structure as needed

---

## What we value from Élow

This is not a one-sided arrangement. Élow brings things that matter to us, and we want to name them clearly.

- **A warm, established audience.** Élow's client base consists of business owners who care about brand, quality, and professional presentation. That is exactly who Vyager builds for.
- **Credibility as a co-presenter.** When we sit in a sales meeting alongside Rebeca, the trust Élow has built with that client transfers. That is not a small thing.
- **Direct referral potential.** Every Élow client who needs a website, a landing page, or a digital presence is a potential Vyager client. That pipeline has real value.
- **A complementary service set.** Élow handles visual identity. Vyager handles digital infrastructure and traffic. Together the offer to a client is complete. Separately it is not.
- **Rebeca's commercial knowledge.** Her understanding of her clients — their personalities, their hesitations, their goals — makes our involvement in sales meetings more effective than it would be cold.

---

## What we are proposing

**Retainer: R$2,000/month**

The retainer covers the full scope of work listed above. This includes all commercial and sales support. Vyager does not take a commission on deals Élow closes from its own pipeline. The retainer is the complete fee for everything we do inside Élow's business.

To put this in context: hiring an SDR and a marketing manager separately in the Brazilian market would cost between R$3,500 and R$5,000 per month before commission. What we offer covers both those roles, plus strategic direction, for R$2,000 flat.

**Referrals from Élow to Vyager: 10% / 12%**

When Élow refers a client to Vyager and that client closes a paid project, Élow receives a referral commission on the first project only — not on any retainer or ongoing work that follows:

- **10%** on projects under R$8,000
- **12%** on projects of R$8,000 and above

Commission is calculated on the net invoiced amount and paid within 14 days of Vyager receiving payment from the client.

These rates are fixed. They do not change without written agreement from both parties.

**Websites and landing pages: wholesale pricing**

For Élow clients who need a website or landing page, Vyager provides a fixed wholesale price. Élow marks up to the client at whatever rate makes sense for their relationship. No commission calculation. No complexity. Vyager invoices Élow; Élow invoices the client.

---

## What we are asking from this conversation

- Agreement on the retainer continuing from July at R$2,000/month
- Confirmation of the tiered referral rate from Élow to Vyager, documented going forward
- A shared understanding of what each side brings — and that both sides benefit

We are not looking to extract more from this collaboration. We are looking to build on what is working, with terms that both parties understand and have signed off on.

## Next step

If this structure makes sense to both sides, we formalise it in a one-page agreement effective July 2026. Simple, clear, and something we do not have to revisit every few months.
