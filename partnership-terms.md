# Vyager Digital × Élow Estúdio Design — Partnership Terms

**Effective date:** July 2026  
**Prepared by:** Vyager Digital  
**Reviewed with:** Élow Estúdio Design

---

## What this document is

This is a working agreement between Vyager Digital and Élow Estúdio Design. It replaces any previous verbal arrangements and sets a clear structure for both parties going forward. The intent is to make the collaboration sustainable, transparent, and worthwhile on both sides.

---

## Monthly Retainer

**Amount:** R$2,000/month  
**Billing:** Monthly, beginning July 2026

The retainer covers the full scope of Vyager's involvement in Élow's commercial operation. This includes:

- Google Ads campaign management and ongoing optimisation
- Lead monitoring and WhatsApp follow-up with incoming leads
- Meeting scheduling and pipeline management
- Participation in sales meetings alongside the Élow team
- Proposal structure, sales scripts, and commercial strategy support
- Monthly performance reviews and strategic direction

There are no additional fees charged on top of the retainer for any of the above. Vyager does not take a commission on deals Élow closes from its own pipeline. The retainer is the full and only payment for this scope of work.

---

## Referral Commission — Élow to Vyager

When Élow refers a client to Vyager Digital for any service (websites, landing pages, CRM, traffic management), and that client closes a paid engagement with Vyager, Élow receives **10% of the invoiced value** of that engagement.

This commission:

- Applies to the first closed project only, not ongoing retainer payments that may follow
- Is calculated on the net invoiced amount (before transaction fees)
- Is paid to Élow within 14 days of Vyager receiving payment from the client
- Is not subject to change without written agreement from both parties

---

## Website and Landing Page Services — Wholesale Pricing

For clients of Élow who require a website or landing page and where Élow is facilitating the sale, Vyager operates on a wholesale pricing model.

Vyager provides Élow with fixed wholesale prices for each service tier. Élow sets the client-facing price independently and retains the margin. No commission calculation is required. Vyager invoices Élow at the wholesale rate; Élow invoices the client directly.

Wholesale pricing is provided in a separate one-page rate card and updated when Vyager's base costs change, with 30 days notice.

---

## What is not included

The following fall outside this agreement and would require a separate conversation:

- Ongoing traffic management for Élow's own clients (referred through to Vyager)
- CRM setup or automation for Élow internally
- Content creation or social media management

---

## Term and renewal

This agreement runs month-to-month from July 2026. Either party may propose changes to the terms with 30 days written notice. Either party may exit the arrangement with 30 days notice.

The intent is long-term. This structure exists to protect both sides, not to create an exit mechanism.

---

## A note on the collaboration

Vyager has taken on a level of commercial involvement in Élow's business that goes beyond standard marketing management. That involvement has grown deliberately because the results justify it. The structure above reflects that reality — a flat retainer that covers broad operational support, with clean referral terms that reward Élow when they bring business to Vyager.

The goal is a partnership where both businesses grow and neither side feels like the arrangement only benefits the other.
